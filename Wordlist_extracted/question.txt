n	A sentence, phrase or word which asks for information, reply or response; an interrogative. 
n	A subject or topic for consideration or investigation. 
n	A doubt or challenge about the truth, accuracy, or validity of a matter. 
n	A proposal to a meeting as a topic for deliberation. 
n	(now archaic, historical, chiefly with definite article) Interrogation by torture. 
n	(obsolete) Talk; conversation; speech. 
v	(transitive) To ask questions of; to interrogate; to ask for information. 
v	(transitive) To raise doubts about; have doubts about. 
v	(intransitive) To ask a question or questions; inquire or seek to know; examine. 
v	(intransitive, obsolete) To argue; to converse; to dispute.
