n	A speaking or notice of anything, usually in a brief or cursory manner. Used especially in the phrase make mention of. 
n	(Internet, plural only) A social media feed, a list of replies or posts mentioning a person. 
v	To make a short reference to something. 
v	(philosophy, linguistics) To utter a word or expression in order to refer to the expression itself, as opposed to its usual referent.
