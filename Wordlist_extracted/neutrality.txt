n	The state or quality of being neutral; the condition of being unengaged in contests between others; state of taking no part on either side. 
n	(obsolete) Indifference in quality; a state neither very good nor bad. 
n	(chemistry): The quality or state of being neutral. 
n	(international law) The condition of a nation or government which refrains from taking part, directly or indirectly, in a war between other powers. 
n	Those who are neutral; a combination of neutral powers or states.
