v	(ditransitive) To pass on knowledge to. 
v	(intransitive, stative) To pass on knowledge generally, especially as one's profession; to act as a teacher. 
v	(ditransitive) To cause (someone) to learn or understand (something). 
v	(ditransitive) To cause to know the disagreeable consequences of some action. 
v	(obsolete, transitive) To show (someone) the way; to guide, conduct; to point, indicate. 
n	(informal, usually as a term of address) teacher 
n	(slang) Nickname for a teacher. 
n	A surname. Most commonly associated with the pirate Blackbeard, who gave his real name as Edward Teach, Thatch, or Tack.
