n	A conversation or other form of discourse between two or more individuals. 
n	(authorship) In a dramatic or literary presentation, the verbal parts of the script or text; the verbalizations of the actors or characters. 
n	(philosophy) A literary form, where the presentation resembles a conversation. 
n	(computing) A dialogue box. 
v	(informal, business) To discuss or negotiate so that all parties can reach an understanding. 
v	(transitive) To put into dialogue form. 
v	(obsolete) To take part in a dialogue; to dialogize.
