v	(intransitive) To rest in a state of reduced consciousness. 
v	(intransitive, of a spinning top or yo-yo) To spin on its axis with no other perceptible motion. 
v	(transitive) To cause (a spinning top or yo-yo) to spin on its axis with no other perceptible motion. 
v	(transitive) To accommodate in beds. 
v	(transitive) To be slumbering in (a state). 
v	(intransitive) To be careless, inattentive, or unconcerned; not to be vigilant; to live thoughtlessly. 
v	(intransitive) To be dead; to lie in the grave. 
v	(intransitive) To be, or appear to be, in repose; to be quiet; to be unemployed, unused, or unagitated; to rest; to lie dormant. 
v	(computing, intransitive) To wait for a period of time without performing any action. 
v	(computing, transitive) To place into a state of hibernation. 
v	(transitive, reflexive) To achieve or make happen by manner of sleep. 
n	(uncountable) The state of reduced consciousness during which a human or animal rests in a daily rhythm. 
n	(countable, informal) An act or instance of sleeping. 
n	(informal, metonymically) A night. 
n	(uncountable) Rheum, crusty or gummy discharge found in the corner of the eyes after waking, whether real or a figurative objectification of sleep (in the sense of reduced consciousness). 
n	A state of plants, usually at night, when their leaflets approach each other and the flowers close and droop, or are covered by the folded leaves. 
n	The hibernation of animals.
