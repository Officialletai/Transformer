n	(chiefly uncountable) The state of being isolated, detached, or separated; the state of being away from other people. 
n	The act of isolating. 
n	(diplomacy, of a country) The state of not having diplomatic relations with other countries (either with most or all other countries, or with specified other countries). 
n	(chemistry) The obtaining of an element from one of its compounds, or of a compound from a mixture 
n	(medicine) The separation of a patient, suffering from a contagious disease, from contact with others (compare: quarantine) 
n	(databases) A database property that determines when and how changes made in one transaction are visible to other concurrent transactions. 
n	(psychology) A Freudian defense mechanism in which a person suppresses a harmful thought from developing into a train of thought.
