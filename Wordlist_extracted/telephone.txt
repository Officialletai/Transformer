n	A telecommunication device (originally mechanical, and now electronic) used for two-way talking with another person (now often shortened to phone). 
n	(Canada, US, uncountable) The game of Chinese whispers. 
n	(Canada, US, uncountable, figuratively) Chinese whispers; a situation in which an initial message has been distorted and misunderstood by being passed from person to person. 
v	(transitive, intransitive) To (attempt to) contact someone using a telephone. 
v	(transitive) To convey (a message) via telephone.
