adv	(interrogative) At what time? At which time? Upon which occasion or circumstance? Used to introduce direct or indirect questions about time. 
adv	At an earlier time and under different, usually less favorable, circumstances. 
adv	(relative) At which, on which, during which: often omitted or replaced with that. 
adv	(fused relative) The time at, on or during which. 
adv	(informal, in definitions or explanations) A circumstance or situation in which. 
n	The time at which something happens.
