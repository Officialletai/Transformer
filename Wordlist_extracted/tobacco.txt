n	(uncountable) Any plant of the genus Nicotiana. 
n	(uncountable) Leaves of Nicotiana tabacum and some other species cultivated and harvested to make cigarettes, cigars, snuff, for smoking in pipes or for chewing. 
n	(countable) A variety of tobacco. 
v	(intransitive) To indulge in tobacco; to smoke. 
v	(transitive) To treat with tobacco.
