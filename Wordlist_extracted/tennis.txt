n	(sports) A sport played by two players (or four in doubles), who alternately strike the ball over a net using racquets. 
n	(dated) A match in this sport. 
n	(obsolete) An earlier game in which a ball is driven to and fro, or kept in motion by striking it with a racquet or with the open hand. 
v	(intransitive, dated) To play tennis. 
v	(transitive) To drive backward and forward like a tennis ball.
