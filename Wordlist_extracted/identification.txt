n	(strictly) The act of identifying (i.e., which one, which thing). 
n	(broadly, often) The combination of identifying (which one, which thing) and validating the identity (proving or confirming it); identifying and authenticating viewed as a unitary concept in cognition. 
n	The state of being identified. 
n	(countable) A particular instance of identifying something. 
n	A document or documents serving as evidence of a person's identity. 
n	A feeling of support, sympathy, understanding, or belonging towards somebody or something.
