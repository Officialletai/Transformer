adj	Suitable or fit; proper. 
adj	Suitable to the social situation or to social respect or social discreetness; socially correct; socially discreet; well-mannered; proper. 
adj	(obsolete) Set apart for a particular use or person; reserved. 
v	(transitive) To take to oneself; to claim or use, especially as by an exclusive right. 
v	(transitive) To set apart for, or assign to, a particular person or use, especially in exclusion of all others; with to or for. 
v	(transitive, Britain, ecclesiastical, law) To annex (for example a benefice, to a spiritual corporation, as its property). 
v	(transitive, archaic) To make suitable to; to suit.
