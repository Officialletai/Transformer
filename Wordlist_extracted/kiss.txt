v	(transitive) To touch with the lips or press the lips against, usually to show love or affection or passion, or as part of a greeting. 
v	(transitive, intransitive) To (cause to) touch lightly or slightly; to come into contact. 
v	(intransitive) Of two or more people, to touch each other's lips together, usually to express love or affection or passion. 
v	(transitive, archaic) To treat with fondness. 
n	A touch with the lips, usually to express love or affection, or as a greeting. 
n	An 'X' mark placed at the end of a letter or other type of message, signifying the bestowal of a kiss from the sender to the receiver. 
n	A type of filled chocolate candy, shaped as if someone had kissed the top. See Hershey's Kisses. 
n	(astronomy) The alignment of two bodies in the solar system such that they have the same longitude when seen from Earth, conjunction. 
n	(aviation) A low-speed mid-air collision between the envelopes of two hot air balloons, generally causing no damage or injury. 
n	The KISS principle. 
n	A surname from Hungarian. 
v	Acronym of keep it simple, stupid.
