n	(pathology) An abnormal condition of a human, animal or plant that causes discomfort or dysfunction; distinct from injury insofar as the latter is usually instantaneously acquired. 
n	(by extension) Any abnormal or harmful condition, as of society, people's attitudes, way of living etc. 
n	Lack of ease; uneasiness; trouble; vexation; disquiet. 
v	(obsolete) To cause unease; to annoy, irritate. 
v	To infect with a disease.
