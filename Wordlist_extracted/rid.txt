adj	Released from an obligation, problem, etc. (usually followed by of). 
v	(transitive) To free (something) from a hindrance or annoyance. 
v	(transitive, chiefly obsolete) To banish. 
v	(transitive, obsolete) To kill. 
n	Acronym of recognition, intrusion and distraction: three reasons why a lifeguard may fail to notice a person drowning. Specifically: they may fail to recognise the instinctive drowning response; they may have additional duties that intrude on lifeguarding; and they may be distracted. [The act of recognizing or the condition of being recognized (matching a current observation with a memory of a prior observation of the same entity).]
