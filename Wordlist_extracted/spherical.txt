adj	(geometry) Shaped like a sphere. 
adj	(geometry, not comparable) Of, or pertaining to, spheres. 
adj	(mathematics) Of a coordinate system, specifying the location of a point in a plane by using a radius and two angles. 
adj	(astrology) Of or relating to the heavenly orbs, or to the sphere or spheres in which, according to ancient astronomy and astrology, they were set.
