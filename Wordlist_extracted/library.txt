n	An institution which holds books and/or other forms of media for use by the public or qualified people often lending them out, as well as providing various other services for its users. 
n	(by extension) Any institution that lends out its goods for use by the public or a community. 
n	A collection of books or other forms of stored information. 
n	An equivalent collection of analogous information in a non-printed form, e.g. record library. 
n	A room dedicated to storing books. 
n	(programming) A collection of software routines that provide functionality to be incorporated into or used by a computer program. 
n	(genetics) A collection of DNA material from a single organism or relative to a single disease. 
n	(card games) The deck or draw pile.
