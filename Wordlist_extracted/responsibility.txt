n	The state of being responsible, accountable, or answerable. [from 18th c.] 
n	The state of being liable, culpable, or responsible for something in particular. 
n	A duty, obligation or liability for which someone is held accountable. 
n	(military) The obligation to carry forward an assigned task to a successful conclusion. With responsibility goes authority to direct and take the necessary action to ensure success. 
n	(military) The obligation for the proper custody, care, and safekeeping of property or funds entrusted to the possession or supervision of an individual.
