v	(transitive) To meet (someone) or find (something), especially unexpectedly. 
v	(transitive) To confront (someone or something) face to face. 
v	(transitive, intransitive) To engage in conflict, as with an enemy. 
v	(transitive, India) To execute someone extrajudicially. 
n	A meeting, especially one that is unplanned or unexpected. 
n	A hostile, often violent meeting; a confrontation, skirmish, or clash, as between combatants. 
n	(sports) A match between two opposing sides. 
n	(sexuality) A sexual encounter; sexual activity, especially unplanned or unexpected, between people not in a sexual relationship, that usually does not lead to the establishment of a relationship, and may or may not happen again. A sexual encounter could be consensual or non-consensual; in the latter case, it is a sexual assault. A consensual sexual encounter that happens only once is commonly known as a one-night stand. 
n	(India) An extrajudicial killing or execution.
