v	(transitive, intransitive) To be undecided about; to lack confidence in; to disbelieve, to question. 
v	(transitive, archaic) To harbour suspicion about; suspect. 
v	(transitive, archaic) To anticipate with dread or fear; to apprehend. 
v	(transitive, obsolete) To fill with fear; to affright. 
v	(transitive, intransitive, obsolete) To dread, to fear. 
n	(uncountable, countable) Disbelief or uncertainty (about something); (countable) a particular instance of such disbelief or uncertainty. 
n	(countable, obsolete or India) A point of uncertainty; a query.
