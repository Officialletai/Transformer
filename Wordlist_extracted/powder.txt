n	The fine particles which are the result of reducing a dry substance by pounding, grinding, or triturating, or the result of decay; dust. 
n	(cosmetics) A mixture of fine dry, sweet-smelling particles applied to the face or other body parts, to reduce shine or to alleviate chaffing. 
n	An explosive mixture used in gunnery, blasting, etc.; gunpowder. 
v	(transitive) To reduce to fine particles; to pound, grind, or rub into a powder. 
v	(transitive) To sprinkle with powder, or as if with powder. 
v	(intransitive) To use powder on the hair or skin. 
v	(intransitive) To turn into powder; to become powdery. 
v	(obsolete, transitive) To sprinkle with salt; to corn, as meat. 
v	(intransitive, slang) To depart suddenly; to "take a powder". 
n	A surname. 
n	(informal) Ellipsis of powder snow.; light, dry, fluffy snow. [Freshly fallen, uncompacted snow, especially in the context of Alpine skiing] 
n	Ellipsis of powder blue.; the colour powder blue. [A very pale blue colour.]
