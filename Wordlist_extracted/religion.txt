n	(uncountable) Belief in a spiritual or metaphysical reality (often including at least one deity), accompanied by practices or rituals pertaining to the belief. 
n	(countable) A particular system of such belief, and the rituals and practices proper to it. 
n	(uncountable) The way of life committed to by monks and nuns. 
n	(uncountable, informal) Rituals and actions associated with religious beliefs, but considered apart from them. 
n	(countable) Any practice to which someone or some group is seriously devoted. 
n	(uncountable, obsolete) Faithfulness to a given principle; conscientiousness. 
v	Engage in religious practice. 
v	Indoctrinate into a specific religion. 
v	To make sacred or symbolic; sanctify.
