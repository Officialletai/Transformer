n	(chiefly uncountable) Capacity of mind, especially to understand principles, truths, facts or meanings, acquire knowledge, and apply it to practice; the ability to comprehend and learn. 
n	(chiefly uncountable) The quality of making use or having made use of such capacities: depth of understanding, mental quickness. 
n	(countable) An entity that has such capacities. 
n	(chiefly uncountable) Information, often secret, about an enemy or about hostile activities. 
n	(countable) A political or military department, agency or unit designed to gather information, usually secret, about the enemy or about hostile activities. 
n	(dated) Acquaintance; intercourse; familiarity.
