n	A loud, emphatic, exclamation of extreme emotion, especially horror, fear, excitement, or anger; it may comprise a word or a sustained, high-pitched vowel sound. 
n	A loud vocalisation of many animals, especially in response to pain or fear. 
n	(music) A form of singing associated with the metal and screamo styles of music. It is a loud, rough, distorted version of the voice; rather than the normal voice of the singer. 
n	(informal) Used as an intensifier. 
n	(printers' slang) An exclamation mark. 
v	(intransitive, also figuratively) To cry out with a shrill voice; to utter a sudden, shout outcry, or shrill, loud cry, as in fright or extreme pain; to screech, to shriek. 
v	To move quickly; to race. 
v	(informal) To be very indicative of; clearly having the characteristics of.
