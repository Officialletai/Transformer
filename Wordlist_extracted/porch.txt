n	(architecture) A covered entrance to a building, whether taken from the interior, and forming a sort of vestibule within the main wall, or projecting without and with a separate roof. A porch often has chair(s), table(s) and swings. 
n	A portico; a covered walk. 
n	The platform outside the external hatch of a spacecraft. 
n	A surname. 
n	(with definite article) The Stoic philosophy (after the public porch on the agora of Athens where Zeno taught).
