n	(uncountable) The process of adapting something or becoming adapted to a situation; adjustment, modification. 
n	(countable) A change that is made or undergone to suit a condition or environment. 
n	(uncountable, evolutionary theory) The process of change that an organism undergoes to be better suited to its environment. 
n	(countable, evolutionary theory) An instance of an organism undergoing change, or the structure or behavior that is changed. 
n	(uncountable) The process of adapting an artistic work from a different medium. 
n	(countable, authorship) An artistic work that has been adapted from a different medium. 
n	(sociology) The means by which social groups adapt to different social and physical environments.
