n	An item of clothing, covering all or part of the hand and fingers, but usually allowing independent movement of the fingers. 
n	A baseball mitt. 
n	(baseball, figuratively) The ability to catch a hit ball. 
n	(slang) A condom. 
n	(with definite article) A challenge from one to another. 
v	(baseball, transitive) To catch the ball in a baseball mitt. 
v	(transitive) To put a glove or gloves on. 
v	(cricket) To touch a delivery with one's glove while the gloved hand is on the bat. Under the rules of cricket, the batsman is deemed to have hit the ball.
