n	A hundred-dollar bill, or any other note denominated 100 (e.g. a hundred euros). 
n	(historical) An administrative subdivision of southern English counties formerly reckoned as comprising 100 hides (households or families) and notionally equal to 12,000 acres. 
n	(by extension, historical) Similar divisions in other areas, particularly in other areas of Britain or the British Empire 
n	(cricket) A score of one hundred runs or more scored by a batsman.
