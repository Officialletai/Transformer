n	The striking of one body against another; collision. 
n	The force or energy of a collision of two objects. 
n	(chiefly medicine) A forced impinging. 
n	(figuratively, proscribed) A significant or strong influence or effect. 
v	(transitive) To collide or strike, the act of impinging. 
v	(transitive) To compress; to compact; to press into something or pack together. 
v	(transitive, figuratively, proscribed) To significantly or strongly influence or affect; to have an impact on. 
v	(transitive, rare) To stamp or impress onto something.
