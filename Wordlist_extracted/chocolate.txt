n	(chiefly uncountable) A food made from ground roasted cocoa beans. 
n	(chiefly uncountable) A drink made by dissolving this food in boiling milk or water. 
n	(countable) A single, small piece of confectionery made from chocolate. 
n	(uncountable) A dark, reddish-brown colour/color, like that of chocolate (also called chocolate brown). 
n	(countable, slang) A black person; (uncountable) blackness. 
adj	Made of or containing chocolate. 
adj	Having a dark reddish-brown colour/color. 
adj	(slang) Black (relating to any of various ethnic groups having dark pigmentation of the skin). 
v	(transitive, rare, chiefly in the past participle) To add chocolate to; to cover (food) in chocolate. 
v	(rare, biology) To treat blood agar by heating in order to lyse the red blood cells in the medium.
