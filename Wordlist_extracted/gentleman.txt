n	(chiefly historical) A man of gentle but not noble birth, particularly a man of means (originally ownership of property) who does not work for a living but has no official status in a peerage; (UK law) an armiferous man ranking below a knight. 
n	Any well-bred, well-mannered, or charming man. 
n	(derogatory) An effeminate or oversophisticated man. 
n	(polite term of address) Any man. 
n	(usually historical, sometimes derogatory) An amateur or dabbler in any field, particularly those of independent means. 
n	(cricket) An amateur player, particularly one whose wealth permits him to forego payment. 
n	A surname transferred from the nickname.
