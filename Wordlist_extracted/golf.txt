n	(sports) A ball game played by individuals competing against one another in which the object is to hit a ball into each of a series of (usually 18 or nine) holes in the minimum number of strokes. 
v	(intransitive) To play the game of golf. 
v	(computing) To write something in as few characters as possible (e.g. in code golf, regex golf) 
n	(international standards) Alternative letter-case form of Golf from the NATO/ICAO Phonetic Alphabet.
