n	A spoken or written remark. 
n	(uncountable) The act of commenting. 
n	(linguistics) The part of a sentence that provides new information regarding the current theme. 
n	(programming) A remark embedded in source code in such a way that it will be ignored by the compiler or interpreter, typically to help people to understand the code. 
v	(transitive) To remark. 
v	(intransitive, with "on" or "about") To make remarks or notes. 
v	(transitive, obsolete) To comment or remark on. 
v	(transitive, software, of code) To insert comments into (source code). 
v	(transitive, software, of code) To comment out (code); to disable by converting into a comment.
