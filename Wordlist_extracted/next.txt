adj	Nearest in place or position, having nothing similar intervening; adjoining. 
adj	(obsolete) Most direct, or shortest or nearest in distance or time. 
adj	Nearest in order, succession, or rank; immediately following (or sometimes preceding) in order. 
adj	(chiefly law) Nearest in relationship. (See also next of kin.) 
adv	In a time, place, rank or sequence closest or following. 
adv	(conjunctive) So as to follow in time or sequence something previously mentioned. 
adv	On the first subsequent occasion. 
n	The one that follows after this one.
