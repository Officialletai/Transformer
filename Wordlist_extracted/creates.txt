v	(especially of a god) To bring into existence out of nothing, without the prior existence of the materials or elements used. 
v	To make or produce from other (e.g. raw, unrefined or scattered) materials or combinable elements or ideas; to design or invest with a new form, shape, function, etc. 
v	(transitive) To cause, to bring (a non-object) about by an action, behavior, or event, to occasion. 
v	(transitive) To confer or invest with a rank or title of nobility, to appoint, ordain or constitute. 
v	(intransitive) To be or do something creative, imaginative, originative. 
v	(transitive) In theatre, to be the first performer of a role; to originate a character. 
v	(UK, intransitive, colloquial) To make a fuss, complain; to shout. 
adj	(obsolete) Created, resulting from creation.
