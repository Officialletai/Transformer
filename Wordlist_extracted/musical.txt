adj	Of, belonging or relating to music, or to its performance or notation. 
adj	Pleasing to the ear; sounding agreeably; having the qualities of music; melodious; harmonious. 
adj	Fond of music; discriminating with regard to music; gifted or skilled in music. 
adj	Pertaining to a class of games in which players move while music plays, but have to take a fixed position when it stops; by extension, any situation where people repeatedly change positions. 
n	A stage performance, show or film that involves singing, dancing and musical numbers performed by the cast as well as acting. 
n	(probably archaic or obsolete) A meeting or a party for a musical entertainment; a musicale.
