adj	(not comparable) Beyond what is due, usual, expected, or necessary; extraneous; additional; supernumerary. 
adj	(not comparable, dated) Extraordinarily good; superior. 
adj	(comparable, slang) Over the top; going beyond what is normal or appropriate, often in a dramatic manner. 
adv	(informal) To an extraordinary degree. 
n	Something additional, such as an item above and beyond the ordinary school curriculum, or added to the usual charge on a bill. 
n	An extra edition of a newspaper, which is printed outside of the normal printing cycle, for example to report an important late-breaking event. 
n	(cricket) A run scored without the ball having hit the striker's bat - a wide, bye, leg bye or no ball. 
n	(acting) A supernumerary or walk-on in a film or play. 
n	(slang) The state or trait of being over the top, of behaving in an overly dramatic manner. 
n	Something of an extra quality or grade.
