n	(uncountable) The act of speaking, a certain style of it. 
n	(countable) A formal session of speaking, especially a long oral message given publicly by one person.

n	(uncountable) Language used orally, rather than in writing. 
n	(grammar) An utterance that is quoted; see direct speech, reported speech 
n	(uncountable) Public talk, news, gossip, rumour.
