adv	(interrogative) In what way; to what extent. 
adv	Used before a prepositional phrase to emphasise that something is taken into consideration as a cause or reason; usually used in combination with 'with' (see what with), and much less commonly with other prepositions. 
n	(obsolete, uncountable) Something; thing; stuff. 
n	(countable) The identity of a thing, as an answer to a question of what. 
n	(countable) Something that is addressed by what, as opposed to a person, addressed by who.
