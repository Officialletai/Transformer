n	The effect upon the judgment or feelings produced by any event, whether witnessed or participated in; personal and direct impressions as contrasted with description or fancies; personal acquaintance; actual enjoyment or suffering. 
n	(countable) An activity one has performed. 
n	(countable) A collection of events and/or activities from which an individual or group may gather knowledge, opinions, and skills. 
n	(uncountable) The knowledge thus gathered. 
n	(obsolete, uncountable) Trial; a test or experiment. 
v	(transitive) To observe certain events; undergo a certain feeling or process; or perform certain actions that may alter one or contribute to one's knowledge, opinions, or skills.
