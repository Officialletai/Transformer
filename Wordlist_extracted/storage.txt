n	(uncountable) The act of storing goods; the state of being stored. 
n	(usually countable) An object or place in which something is stored. 
n	(usually uncountable, computer hardware) Any computer device, including such as a disk, on which data is stored for a longer term than main memory. 
n	(uncountable) The price charged for storing goods. 
v	To put into storage; to store.
