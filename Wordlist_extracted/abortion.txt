n	(medicine) The expulsion from the womb of a foetus or embryo before it is fully developed, with loss of the foetus; either naturally as a spontaneous abortion (now usually called a miscarriage), or deliberately as an induced abortion. 
n	(now rare) An aborted foetus; an abortus. 
n	(figuratively) A misshapen person or thing; a monstrosity. 
n	(figuratively) Failure or abandonment of a project, promise, goal etc. 
n	(biology) Arrest of development of any organ, so that it remains an imperfect formation or is absorbed. 
n	The cessation of an illness or disease at a very early stage.
