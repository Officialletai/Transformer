n	A ruminant mammal with antlers and hooves of the family Cervidae, or one of several similar animals from related families of the order Artiodactyla. 
n	(in particular) One of the smaller animals of this family, distinguished from a moose or elk 
n	The meat of such an animal; venison. 
n	(obsolete, except in the phrase "small deer") Any animal, especially a quadrupedal mammal as opposed to a bird, fish, etc. 
n	A village in Aberdeenshire, Scotland.
