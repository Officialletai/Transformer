n	The state of being a minor; youth, the period of a person's life prior to reaching adulthood. 
n	Any subgroup that does not form a numerical majority. 
n	(US) A member of an ethnic minority. 
n	(sociology) A group of people seen as distinct who are subordinated and discriminated against in a society. 
adj	(relational, attributive) Of or relating to a minority. 
adj	(attributive, politics, of a party, government, etc.) Empowered by or representing a minority (usually a plurality) of votes cast, legislative seats, etc., rather than an outright majority thereof.
