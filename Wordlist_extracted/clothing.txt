n	Any of a wide variety of articles, usually made of fabrics, animal hair, animal skin, or some combination thereof, used to cover the human body for warmth, to preserve modesty, or for fashion. 
n	An act or instance of putting clothes on. 
n	(obsolete) The art or process of making cloth. 
n	A covering of non-conducting material on the outside of a boiler, or steam chamber, to prevent radiation of heat.
